# Rewards

Every transaction Canton Keeper submits earns Canton Coin from the network's Featured App reward pool.

## How It Works

Under the upcoming traffic-based rewards (CIP-0104), each featured app party on a transaction receives an equal share of the app rewards generated by that transaction. CK doesn't need to create activity markers — rewards are calculated automatically from transaction traffic for any party holding a FeaturedAppRight.

## Who Benefits

**Validators** — every automated transaction earns Canton Coin rewards. More jobs configured means more transactions, more rewards. CK runs 24/7 so you never miss a reward-eligible operation.

**App Developers** — CK generates transactions on your contracts, increasing your app's network activity. Each featured app party on the transaction earns an equal share of the rewards.

**Saxon Nodes** — as a featured app party, Saxon earns an equal share of app rewards on CK transactions. This funds ongoing development, new catalog entries, and ecosystem tooling.

## Reward Distribution

Under traffic-based rewards, app rewards are split equally among all featured app parties on each transaction:

| Featured parties on transaction | Share per party |
|--------------------------------|-----------------|
| 1 (CK only) | 100% |
| 2 (CK + one app) | 50% each |
| 3 (CK + two apps) | 33% each |

Validator rewards (separate from app rewards) are earned by the validator operator regardless of the number of featured app parties.

## Why It Matters Now

The Canton Network's Featured App reward pool significantly favours active applications until mid-2029. Early adoption means a larger share of a growing pool while competition is low.
