# Rewards

Every transaction Canton Keeper submits earns Canton Coin for your validator from the network's reward pool.

## Why Your Validator Earns More With CK

- **Never miss a reward** — without automation, contracts expire unexercised and those rewards go to nobody. CK fires choices the moment conditions are met, 24/7
- **More transactions, more CC** — every automated cancel, settle, execute, and bill is a rewarded transaction your validator wouldn't have submitted manually
- **Zero cost** — CK uses your existing credentials and runs alongside your participant. No additional infrastructure, no fees to Saxon
- **Early adopter advantage** — the Featured App reward pool significantly favours active applications until mid-2029. The earlier you run CK, the larger your share

## How It Works

Under traffic-based rewards (CIP-0104), rewards are calculated automatically from transaction traffic. CK doesn't need to create activity markers. Each featured app party on a transaction receives an equal share of the app rewards generated by that transaction.

## Who Benefits

**Validators** — earn Canton Coin on every automated transaction. More apps installed means more jobs auto-discovered, more transactions, more rewards.

**App Developers** — CK generates transactions on your contracts, increasing your app's network activity. Each featured app party on the transaction earns an equal share of the rewards.

**Saxon Nodes** — as a featured app party, Saxon earns an equal share of app rewards on CK transactions. This funds ongoing development, new catalog entries, and ecosystem tooling.

## Reward Distribution

Under traffic-based rewards, app rewards are split equally among all featured app parties on each transaction:

| Featured parties on transaction | Share per party |
|--------------------------------|-----------------|
| 1 (CK only) | 100% |
| 2 (CK + one app) | 50% each |
| 3 (CK + two apps) | 33% each |

Validator rewards (separate from app rewards) are earned by the validator operator regardless of the number of featured app parties.

## Why It Matters Now

The Canton Network's Featured App reward pool significantly favours active applications until mid-2029. Early adoption means a larger share of a growing pool while competition is low.
