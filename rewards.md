# Rewards

Every transaction Canton Keeper submits earns Canton Coin from the network's Featured App reward pool.

## Who Benefits

**Validators** — earn 80% of CK's app rewards on every automated transaction. More jobs configured means more transactions, more rewards. CK runs 24/7 so you never miss a reward-eligible operation.

**App Developers** — CK generates transactions on your contracts, increasing your app's network activity. CK's rewards are separate from yours — multiple activity markers per transaction are allowed, so both CK and your app earn independently.

**Saxon Nodes** — receives 20% of CK's app rewards as the developer. This funds ongoing development, Canton 3.5 compatibility, and new example configs for the ecosystem.

## Reward Split

| Recipient | Share |
|-----------|-------|
| Validator operator | 80% |
| Saxon Nodes | 20% |

## Why It Matters Now

The Canton Network's Featured App reward pool significantly favours active applications until mid-2029. Early adoption means a larger share of a growing pool while competition is low.
